Booking & Inquiries

How to begin.

How do I book time with you?
The best way to begin is through my booking form. It keeps everything clear, complete, and easy to review. Thoughtful introductions are always appreciated.
What should I include in my inquiry?
Please include your name, requested date, city, length of time, and the kind of experience you have in mind, along with any screening details requested on my booking form. The more complete your inquiry, the easier it is for me to consider it properly.
Do you prefer the booking form or direct email?
The booking form is always preferred. It is the most efficient and organized way to reach me. Direct email is available, but complete details are still expected.
Do you offer a more discreet contact option?
Yes. For more discreet parties, I can also be reached at keikiconnoisseur@protonmail.ch. If you choose that route, please still include everything requested on my booking form. Replies there may take a bit longer.
How long does it take to hear back from you?
Response times vary depending on volume, schedule, and how complete the inquiry is. Thoughtful, complete messages are always easier to prioritize.
Do you respond to all inquiries?
I prioritize messages that are respectful, complete, and clearly aligned. Incomplete or careless inquiries may not receive a response.
Do you accept last-minute requests?
Sometimes, but advance planning is always preferred. The best experiences tend to be the ones arranged with care.

Deposits & Payment

Booking policy.

Is a deposit required?
Yes. A deposit is required to reserve time.
How much is the deposit?
For bookings under 3 hours, the deposit is 30%. For bookings of 3 hours or more, the deposit is 50%. Travel bookings require 50% in advance.
What payment methods do you accept?
Deposits may be sent via Cash App, Venmo, Zelle, or BTC.
Are deposits refundable?
Deposits are non-refundable unless I cancel, in which case the deposit is returned in full.
What happens if I need to cancel?
If a cancellation occurs within 72 hours, 50% of the deposit is owed. If a cancellation occurs within 24 hours, the full deposit is owed.
When is the remaining balance due?
Final details are handled privately once a booking is confirmed.
