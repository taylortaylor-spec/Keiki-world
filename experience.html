Booking Policy

A deposit is required to reserve time.

For bookings under 3 hours, the deposit is 30%.
For bookings of 3 hours or more, the deposit is 50%.
For travel bookings, 50% is required in advance.

Deposits may be sent via Cash App, Venmo, Zelle, or BTC.

Deposits are non-refundable unless I cancel, in which case the deposit is returned in full.

If a cancellation occurs within 72 hours, 50% of the deposit is owed.
If a cancellation occurs within 24 hours, the full deposit is owed.

Compensation is for my time only.
